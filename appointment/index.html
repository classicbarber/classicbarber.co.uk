Appointment Request

Appointments are requests only and should not be considered as confirmed until you have been contacted personally by the shop.

Please give at least 24 hours notice, via telephone, if you are unable to keep your appointment as a full charge will be made for a missed appointment.

Should you wish to make or cancel an appointment, please call rather than email.
