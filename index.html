The Classic Barber of Hertford Town

COVID-19 Update
April 2021

In accordance with the government roadmap out of lockdown rules we shall be open from Tuesday, 13th April 2021.

Please keep listening to national news services for the latest advice and check back here for any new announcements.

Should any of our regular clients wish to make contact please do so by this email link as we are unable to answer the phone at the moment for hygiene reasons. Please note, you must leave the name of your barber on the form.

We are now open. There are a few changes in place to keep both clients and the team safe. PPE will be worn by the team, your temperature will be taken and you will be required to use our hand sanitiser station upon arrival.

Cleaning disinfecting and sterilising will be carried out between each client. Unfortunately this will impact on the number of clients we can see each day, and therefore prices have had to increase to keep the business viable. You will be advised of the price when you book.

Should any of our regular clients wish to make contact please do so by this email link as we are unable to answer the phone at the moment for obvious reasons. Please note, you must leave the name of your barber on the form.

This service is for regular clients only.

We are unable to take new clients at this time.

The Classic Barber Hertford has been providing discerning gentlemen with a quality service for 25 years. Join us in a relaxing environment where we provide an exclusive appointment service. Your time is important, why waste it sitting in long queues.

As well as being Hertfords' stockist of American Crew styling and grooming products, we also retail Mason Pearson products including Shaving brushes, hair brushes and combs along with Mitchell's traditional shaving soap, all which are popular with our clients and compliment our traditional style, with a modern feel, barber shop.

We cater for gentlemen of any age, many of our clients have grown up with us and now bring their own sons with them to experience our passion for hair and to receive our individual, personal service catering for both trends and traditional styles.

We open from Tuesday to Saturday from 8.30 through to 6.00 with Thursday staying open until 7.00. So if you're not happy with your current barber shop, why not give us a call to book an appointment and come and get the haircut you deserve.
