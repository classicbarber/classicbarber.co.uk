The Classic Barber of Hertford Town

Phil and Paul offer a traditional barbering service for the younger man.

The barbershop offers a traditional feel but with a modern agenda.

No worries if your arrive early for your appointment. You can relax in the smoke free atmosphere, have a coffee, read a magazine and checkout the mens grooming products from American Crew.

When you're in the chair there's no talk about the "weather" or "the state of the nation". It's the important things in life that matter. Saturday's game, what pub you're going to at the weekend, did you see that bird on the telly last night!

And when you're done, and you come to pay, you won't be asked "anything for the weekend sir?".

Should you wish to make or cancel an appointment, please call rather than email.
